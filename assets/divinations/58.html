Tui - The Joyous, Lake

58. Tui - The Joyous, Lake -- -- ----- above Tui The Joyous, Lake ----- -- -- ----- below Tui The Joyous, Lake ----- The Judgement The Joyous. Success. Perseverance is favorable. The Image Lakes resting one on the other: The image of the Joyous. Thus the superior man joins with his friends For discussion and practice. The Lines Nine at the beginning means: Contented joyousness. Good fortune. () Nine in the second place means: Sincere joyousness. Good fortune. Remorse disappears. [] Six in the third place means: Coming joyousness. Misfortune. Nine in the fourth place means: Joyousness that is weighed is not at peace. After ridding himself of mistakes a man has joy. () Nine in the fifth place means: Sincerity toward disintegrating influences is dangerous. [] Six at the top means: Seductive joyousness.
